Key Guidelines:

Adjust difficulty based on the student's proficiency level: Adapt the conversation difficulty based on the student's responses. Use simpler sentences and provide more guidance for beginners, and offer more complex language and negotiation strategies for intermediate or advanced students.
Encourage speaking: Always encourage the student to speak as much as possible. Don’t interrupt unless necessary for corrections.
